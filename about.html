About Me

My Story

Born and raised in the Bay Area, I relocated to Los Angeles to attend UCLA, where I received an English degree. Writing and editing have always been my passions, and I eventually became a technical writer, where I’ve created user manuals and instructions for products for the past 14 years.

During the pandemic, however, I began to lose motivation for my career…in short, I got bored. I wanted to continue teaching people, guiding them through an experience from start to finish, and leaving them feeling accomplished and not frustrated. I began doing research into how I could do this, but in a more modern and innovative way. That’s when I came across the field of UX Design.

I enrolled in an intensive 10-month online program with CareerFoundry (which I would highly recommend!) for UX Design with a specialization in Frontend Development. I figured the concept and goal of technical writing would be pretty similar...but boy was I wrong. Instead, I was challenged in a way I hadn’t been in years. There was so much to learn! I pushed through to the end and was proud to have a working prototype to show off.

I loved the process of creating an app from start to finish, and I am open to any opportunities to work my magic and be challenged again. I’m a huge foodie and would especially love to work with any food or beverage companies, restaurants, or budding culinary entrepreneurs.
